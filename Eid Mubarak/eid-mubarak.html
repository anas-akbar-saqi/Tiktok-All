Duas & Blessings

🤲
تَقَبَّلَ اللَّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنْكُمْ

"May Allah accept (good deeds) from us and from you. May He shower your home with His mercy and fill your heart with His light."

☪️
عِيدٌ مُبَارَكٌ

"May Allah accept your prayers, forgive your sins, and fill every day of your life with happiness, peace, and boundless blessings."

✨
كُلَّ عَامٍ وَأَنْتُمْ بِخَيْرٍ

"May every year find you in good health. May Allah's blessings be upon you, your family, and all those you hold dear this Eid."

🕌
اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا

"O Allah, bless us. Grant us Your mercy, guidance, and grace. Let this Eid be a celebration of faith, gratitude, and togetherness."

🌙
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً

"Our Lord, grant us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and protect us from the punishment of the Fire. Ameen."

💫
اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ

"O Allah, You are Pardoning and You love to pardon, so pardon us. May this Eid bring you closer to Allah and to those you love."

A Blessed Message

✦ A Heartfelt Wish ✦
Eid Mubarak
عيد مبارك سعيد
❈

As the crescent moon rises and the sound of takbeer fills the air, may your heart be filled with the warmth of Allah's love and the joy of this sacred celebration. May your fasts be accepted, your prayers answered, and your sins forgiven. May this Eid bring your family closer together, fill your table with abundance, and your life with endless moments of peace, laughter, and gratitude.

You are remembered in our prayers this beautiful day.
Eid Mubarak.
